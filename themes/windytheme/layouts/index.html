What we do

Masterminds

The Ultimate Entrepreneurial Brain Trust

Unlock the true potential of collaboration and problem-solving through our exclusive Mastermind Sessions.

These intimate events bring together a curated group of four entrepreneurs, handpicked to provide invaluable insights and diverse perspectives. Over the course of an hour, you'll dive deep into each other's businesses, offering guidance, support, and innovative solutions for the challenges you face.

Co-Mentorship Groups

The Alchemy of Group Dynamics

Within Foundership, the secret to extraordinary growth lies not only in your individual efforts but also in the collective wisdom of a supportive group. Enter the realm of Co-Mentorship Groups – an invigorating space that encourages knowledge exchange, meaningful discussions, and shared journeys to success.

These dedicated groups meet every three weeks, offering you a consistent forum for ideation and brainstorming, guided by an experienced facilitator. Dive into stimulating conversations about business, life, markets, and opportunities, and explore ways to take your entrepreneurial pursuits to new heights. Each meeting is a collaborative endeavor aimed at driving business progress for everyone involved.

Community Calls

The Pulse of Collective Wisdom

Experience an exchange of ideas that can transform your business trajectory with our Monthly Community Calls. Hosted by Foundership and featuring the brilliant minds of our community, these discussions serve as a hub for exploring the pulse of entrepreneurship.

Together, we delve into a wide array of topics, from new technologies and market trends to investment insights and the latest business events. Each call is an exciting exploration of novel ideas and learnings that can amplify your business's growth and advancement. Listen, learn, participate, and let the collective knowledge of our community guide your success.

Newsletter

The Essential Entrepreneurial Chronicle

Navigate the world of entrepreneurship with greater confidence and insight, courtesy of our monthly newsletter - the Foundership Digest. Designed to keep you in the loop and ahead of the curve, this comprehensive digest encapsulates everything you need to fuel your entrepreneurial journey.

The Foundership Digest is your key to staying updated on the fast-paced world of entrepreneurship. With every edition, we bring you critical updates from the Foundership community, a round-up of noteworthy events, and insights from member businesses. This is your chance to catch up on their triumphs, challenges, and learnings.

But we don’t stop there. Our newsletter is an expansive resource for transformative growth tactics, featuring new learnings to optimize business operations and handpicked growth hacks that will empower your business. From novel technological trends to untapped opportunities, the Foundership Digest keeps you informed, prepared, and inspired.

We know you're busy changing the world, and that's why we deliver a succinct, curated collection of the most impactful insights and news, straight to your inbox each month.

With the Foundership Digest, stay connected, stay informed, and let knowledge be the catalyst for your entrepreneurial success.

Co-Promotion, Content, Collaboration

The Synergy of Shared Success

Experience the power of community-backed promotion and collaboration. Foundership's Co-Promotion, Content, and Collaboration initiative provides a springboard for your business, connecting you with the community's unparalleled network.

Members enjoy the unique advantage of cross-promotion within the community, opening up avenues for collaborative endeavors and sparking innovation. Further, member businesses gain exposure on our extensive public channels, including social media platforms and newsletters.

Join us in this collective journey of success, where your growth is supported and celebrated by a community that genuinely understands and appreciates your entrepreneurial spirit. In the Foundership community, success is a shared story, and every story matters.

Private Communities

Experience the heartbeat of Foundership through our exclusive private Discord server and email list.

Connect with fellow founders 24/7, collaborate in real-time, engage in curated discussions, and gain insider access to resources and opportunities. Our digital community empowers you to learn, grow, and thrive alongside like-minded entrepreneurs.
